Time spent drowning in the depth of the past...

...struggling to grasp the future

All while the moment
sits patiently

Balancing delicately between the yesterday...

...and the tommorrow

Like the peaceful eye
in the middle
of a hurricane

Here in a world full of mountains and clouds,

BIG problems become SMALL

And the possibilities become endless

In each moment exists an indescribable beauty

Where everything is simply as it is
